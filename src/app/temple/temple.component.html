Temple

You can feel an aura of unholy power in the air as you approach the temple. Cautiously, you walk up to the door and proceed inside.

The interior doesn't look much different from what you'd expect, with benches facing an altar. There are a few people scattered throughout the benches, appearing to be in prayer - though to WHAT, you don't know.

A priestess stands at the altar, calling out to you.

Come closer, child.

You approach the altar hesitantly, asking what the purpose of this place is.

For the townsfolk, a place of worship. But for travellers like you? It's a different story.

She motions toward four boxes on the altar. Each box is small, and made of metal, with a slot in the top that appears to be for coins. The boxes are labelled.

Travellers such as yourself often leave coins behind, to help support the work Naomi does. Making a monthly donation can also grant you some special blessings.

Would you like to leave some coins in one of the boxes?
