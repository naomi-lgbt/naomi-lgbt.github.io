It has been a long journey, but you have finally arrived at Avalon, the city that Naomi built. Feeling relieved, you reach into your pack and pull out a map of the area.

Unfortunately, your map isn't very detailed. You aren't quite sure where you should head.

Thankfully, there appears to be a central area in the middle of the city. You can hear some activity coming from that direction. It must be some sort of plaza - surely you could get better directions there!

On the way, you notice an interesting statue of Naomi. This statue appears to be moving. It would seem that there is magic at work here.
