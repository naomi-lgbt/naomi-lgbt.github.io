Welcome

It has been a long journey, but you have finally arrived at Naomi's Domain. Feeling relieved, you reach into your pack and pull out a map of the area, to determine where to go next. There seem to be a few places of interest:

  • Over at the Library, you could read up on Naomi's history and background.
  • Sounds of battle can be heard from the Arena, where Naomi is in frequent battle with bugs.
  • The Tavern would be the place to go to socialise with your fellow travellers.
  • Great works of art can be seen in the Museum.
  • If you wish to see past adventures and brush up on your adventuring abilities, spend some time in the Training Hall.
  • You've heard that people sometimes choose to make a donation to the Temple, so that the work done in this land can continue.
